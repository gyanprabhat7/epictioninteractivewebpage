    • Policies

    • All games published by Epiction must be of high quality and meet our standards for gameplay, graphics, and content.
    • All games must be appropriate for a general audience.
    • All games must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
  •  
    • Intellectual Property
    • You retain ownership of all intellectual property rights in your game.
    • Epiction will have the exclusive right to distribute and monetize your game for a period of years.
    • After the expiration of the exclusivity period, you will have the right to distribute and monetize your game on your own.
  •  

    • Payments

    • Epiction will pay you a royalty on all revenue generated from your game.
    • The royalty rate will be agreed upon in advance and will be based on a number of factors, including the quality of your game, the estimated sales potential, and the cost of marketing and distribution.
    • Payments will be made on a monthly basis, typically within 30 days of the end of the month.
  •  

    • Termination

    • Either party may terminate this agreement at any time for any reason.
    • In the event of termination, Epiction will continue to distribute and monetize your game for a period of months, as agreed upon in advance.
    • After the expiration of the termination period, Epiction will cease all distribution and monetization of your game.
  •  

    • Dispute Resolution

    Any disputes arising out of this agreement will be resolved by binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
